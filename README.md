🌱 Agriconnect Tech4Change - Connecting Farmers with Experts
Helping small-scale farmers grow more with expert advice

📌 What is Agriconnect?
Agriconnect Tech4Change is a friendly digital platform that brings farmers and agricultural experts together. Think of it like a helpful community where farmers can get answers to their questions, share experiences, and learn better farming techniques—all in one place.

Why it matters:
🌾 Farmers can ask questions and get personalized advice from experts

🔍 Find trusted solutions for crop diseases, livestock issues, and more

💡 Learn new farming methods to improve yields

🤝 Connect with other farmers facing similar challenges

✨ Key Features
✅ Ask an Expert – Stuck with a farming problem? Get direct help from agricultural professionals.

✅ Knowledge Hub – Guides, tips, and best practices for better farming.

✅ Community Support – Chat with other farmers, share experiences, and learn together.

✅ Resource Sharing – Access useful tools, government schemes, and farming updates.
